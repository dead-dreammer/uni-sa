How It Works

Your journey to finding the perfect university starts here. Follow our simple process to discover your ideal academic path in South Africa.

Your Path to University Success

1

Create Your Profile

Sign up and tell us about yourself. This helps us create a personalized experience just for you and later on you can save your preferences and get it sent to yoru email for easy access!

2

Answer Smart Questions

Our intelligent system asks targeted questions about your priorities, study preferences, location preferences, and budget. The more details you provide, the better we can match you.

3

Get Personalized Matches

Based on your responses, we generate a curated list of universities and programs that align with your goals. Each match includes detailed information about courses, campus life, and admission requirements.

4

Explore & Compare

Browse through your matches, compare different institutions, check admission dates, and explore available bursaries. Download your personalized report and start preparing for admissions.

5

Take Action

When you're ready, use our resources to apply. Track application deadlines, access bursary information, and get support throughout your application journey.
